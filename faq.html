Frequently Asked Questions

What qualifications does Tiago Santos have as a personal trainer?

Tiago holds both a Bachelor's and a Master's degree in Sport Sciences. With extensive experience and a proven track record of helping clients achieve their fitness goals, he brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to his training sessions.

What types of memberships are available and what do they include?

Tiago offers various membership plans tailored to different needs, including basic, standard, and premium. Each plan includes personalized workout routines, nutritional guidance, and access to consultation videocalls, with the frequency of consultations increasing with higher-tier memberships.

How do I schedule a consultation videocall with Tiago?

Once you've chosen and signed up for a membership plan, you can easily schedule a consultation videocall through the members' portal on Tiago's website. The scheduling system is flexible to accommodate your availability and ensure you get the support you need.

Can Tiago help with injury recovery and specialized training needs?

Absolutely. Tiago specializes in creating customized training programs for clients recovering from injuries, ensuring they can safely return to their fitness routines. He also offers specialized training plans for various fitness goals, from weight loss to muscle building and overall wellness.
