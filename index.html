Welcome to Your Personal Training Journey

Tiago Santos, a dedicated personal trainer hailing from the picturesque island of Madeira, has carved a niche for himself in the vibrant city of Oslo. His journey began with a deep passion for sports, leading him to earn both a Bachelor’s and a Master’s in Sport Sciences. With a solid educational foundation, Tiago set out to transform lives through fitness. Upon moving to Oslo, Tiago’s reputation quickly grew, thanks to his unique ability to connect with clients on a personal level. His extensive experience is peppered with inspiring success stories, from athletes overcoming injuries to everyday individuals reclaiming their strength and vitality. Tiago’s clients often find themselves surprised at the progress they make under his guidance, whether it’s through personalized workout plans or his motivational coaching style.

Memberships

Diet Plan

The Abel app's diet plan feature offers personalized nutrition guidance tailored to individual health goals and preferences. Users start by inputting their dietary needs, health conditions, and goals. Abel then generates customized meal plans, complete with recipes and grocery lists. The app provides daily reminders, tracks nutritional intake, and offers tips for healthy eating. Users can also consult with dietitians through the app for additional support, ensuring they stay on track and make informed dietary choices. This seamless integration of technology and expert advice makes maintaining a healthy diet easier and more effective.

Detailed Training Plan

Abel's training feature delivers personalized workout plans designed to meet users' fitness goals and levels. Users begin by setting their fitness objectives and preferences. The app then creates tailored exercise routines, complete with detailed instructions and video demonstrations. Abel tracks progress, offers real-time feedback, and adjusts plans based on performance and feedback. Users can also connect with personal trainers for virtual sessions and additional guidance. This dynamic approach ensures an engaging and effective fitness journey, helping users stay motivated and achieve their goals efficiently.
