About Me

Tiago Santos, a dedicated personal trainer hailing from the picturesque island of Madeira, has carved a niche for himself in the vibrant city of Oslo. His journey began with a deep passion for sports, leading him to earn both a Bachelor's and a Master's in Sport Sciences. With a solid educational foundation, Tiago set out to transform lives through fitness. Upon moving to Oslo, Tiago's reputation quickly grew, thanks to his unique ability to connect with clients on a personal level. His extensive experience is peppered with inspiring success stories, from athletes overcoming injuries to everyday individuals reclaiming their strength and vitality. Tiago's clients often find themselves surprised at the progress they make under his guidance, whether it's through personalized workout plans or his motivational coaching style. One such story is of Maria, a young mother who had almost given up on her fitness journey due to chronic back pain. With Tiago's expert guidance, she not only regained her strength but also found herself participating in activities she had long thought impossible. Tiago Santos isn’t just a personal trainer; he’s a beacon of hope and transformation, lighting the way for many to rediscover their potential and lead healthier, happier lives.
